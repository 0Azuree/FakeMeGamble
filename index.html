How To Play

Blackjack Rules

The goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11, and others are their face value.

You can "Hit" to take another card or "Stand" to keep your hand. If you go over 21, you lose. The winner is the one with the higher score without busting.
