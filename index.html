Blackjack Rules

The goal is to have a hand with a value as close to 21 as possible without going over. Cards are valued as follows:

  • **2-10:** Face value.
  • **Jack, Queen, King:** 10 points.
  • **Ace:** 1 or 11 points (player's choice).

You and the dealer are dealt two cards. You can choose to "Hit" (take another card) or "Stand" (keep your current hand).

If your hand value goes over 21, you bust and lose the bet. If the dealer busts, you win. The winner is the one with the higher score without busting.
